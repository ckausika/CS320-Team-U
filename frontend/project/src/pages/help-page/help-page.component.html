Finding Research Opportunities

Under the Research tab, there will be a list of posted research positions that will show you the professor that is conducting the research and a description of the lab.

Finding Specific Professors

Under the professors tab, there will be a display of all the professors that are conducting research and what the research topic is.

How to Apply to a Research Opportunity

After finding a research area that you would like to through the research tab, you can click on the Opportunity button and click apply. Then imput your information you can click on the apply button for that opportunity. Then input your information and hit submit and you are finished!

How to Create a Research Opportunity

For the professors that would like to create a research opportunity, They can create an research opportunity on the opportunity tab and under the create section, they can fill out the nessesary information to create a reasearch opportunity. can fill out the necessary information to create a reasearch opportunity.
